    • About Us

    Ayala Land, Inc. is one of the largest property developers in the Philippines.
    We create master-planned communities that promote sustainable development
    and provide long-term value to our residents, investors, and stakeholders.

    Our Services

    Residential Developments

    Ayala Lands develops residential communities ranging from high-end luxury homes
    to affordable housing. Their residential brands include
    Ayala Land Premier, Alveo, Avida, Amaia, and BellaVita, catering to differnt market segments.

    Commercial Leasing

    Ayala Land develops and leases commercial properties such as office buildings, retail spaces, and mixed-use developments.
    Their commercial properties are strategically located in key business district and emerging growth centers across the Philippines.

    Property Management

    Ayala Land provides comprehensive property management services through its subsidiary,
    Ayala Property Management Corporation (APMC). This includes managing residential, commercial, and mixed-use properties to ensure they are well-maintained and operate efficiently.
